About Us

EL TOUR & ORGANIZER is a service company engaged in Tour, Travel and Event Organizer. As a service company we also carry the concept of “ONE STOP SOLUTION”, we have collaborated with several supporting vendors both in Tour activities, and Event Organizers. We also continue to develop alternative tours that are different from many other service companies, such as working with the Indonesian Tour Guide Association, the Indonesian Tourism Ambassador Association, and several professional trainers in the field of Outbound. In this case we as a Tour Travel and Event Organizer service company, can accommodate everything needed including Tour Planning, Employee Gathering, Outbound and all events related to Travel and Event Organizer we will pack systematically, structured, and fun. In accordance with our MOTTO “SAFETY, COMFORT and YOUR SATISFACTION IS A PRIORITY FOR US”.
